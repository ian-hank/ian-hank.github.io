About Myself

Hello everyone! My name is Ian Charles Hank and I am a 20 year old guy from Pennsylvania. I am a mostly self taught programmer with some schooling. I fell in love with computer science back in middle school and have been trying to make that my career since. I began just writing Hello World in a bunch of different languages and my interest grew from there. My best known language is Java. My only real schooling comes from West Virginia University where I have taken some engineering classes as well as multiple Computer Science classes. The primary language taught was Java so a good portion of my learning started there.

I also got very interested in the web design aspect of programming. I taught myself HTML5, CSS3, and some JS. I knew many people who wanted their own website plus I could make it cheap or even free just because I would enjoy designing them. Free practice anyways right? I began designing and finishing multiple, high quality, modern front end designed sites. This included hosting in different ways as well.
